ABOUT

For those doing scientific research, it can be a long time to wait until your writing is ready to publish in full scientific journals. Our platform lets you publish small articles about your research monthly. This means you can publish small chunks of your research and publish them to the web.

Idea Conception

Think of something in your field you'd like to write about.

Compose Article

Write. The more informal, the better. We're looking for engaging ideas and articles, not academic tomes.

Submit For Review

Optionally, you can have your work submitted for feedback from peers before it goes live.

Publish

Once you're ready, publish what you've written for all the world to read.
